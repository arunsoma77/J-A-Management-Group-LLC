JAY PATEL

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

As the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of J&A Management, Jay Patel brings over 10 years of accounting and auditing experience, as well as extensive knowledge of hospitality financial operations. He has expertly managed accounts in industries such as hospitality, real estate, liquor, private equity, gas station, distributors, and government during his tenure.

He also ensures that budgets are completed and submitted in accordance with schedule. As the CFO, he is responsible for investment acquisitions and dispositions, property development, and deployment. Furthermore, he works on finance, accounting, treasury, and administrative assignments for the company. He is a proven executive and leader with over 10 years of experience in the real estate industry.

Our Foundation

Established in 2021, J&A Management Group LLC was founded on principles of integrity, growth, and community. As a dedicated Administrative and Support Services firm, our mission is to provide the critical framework that fosters sustainable growth and operational excellence in every venture we oversee.

Our lean and experienced leadership team provides the strategic vision necessary for our motels, liquor stores, and restaurants to not only succeed but to become pillars in their local communities. We believe in building lasting value for our partners, employees, and customers through focused, expert management.
